Contemporary Catholic Worship

Invigorate your service with accessible, modern worship music.

Hi, I'm Eric.

I'm a composer, educator, web developer, and worship leader. I'm the Director of Contemporary Worship at St. Theresa Catholic Church, and since 2019 I've been writing liturgical music to fill what I saw as a gap in the resources available to Catholic musicians. Putting the label "contemporary" aside, I've written over 100 diverse pieces which I believe can enrich your Mass and empower your congregation to participate fully in the sacrament.

I live in Houston, Texas with my fiancé Stephanie and our dog Bella. I grew up in North Carolina, received degrees in Music and Education at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, and moved to Houston in 2015. When I'm not composing or performing, I enjoy playing basketball, spending time in nature, and traveling. I've visited 20 countries and more than half of the U.S. National Parks.

I'm passionate about music, teaching, and making a positive difference in the world. I hope my music connects with you and may God bless you and your community.

Compositions

Mass Settings

The Mass of Abundant Love is a contemporary mass setting which captures the jubilation and reverence of Catholic worship. Appropriate for Ordinary Time, Christmas, and Easter seasons; the Mass of Abundant Love is a singable, powerful expression of the joy and mystery of the Mass.

Responsorial Psalms

This was my first goal as a Catholic composer. To create responsorial psalms that were singable, powerful, and faithful to the text. Each one has been used in my own service and I'm proud to say our congregation sings these as loud as any song or hymn we use.

The verses are written to meaningfully convey the message of the text while still remaining simple enough for a cantor to learn week to week. The melody is consistent between verses, and the range is accessible for all singers.

I've written a responsorial psalm for nearly every Sunday in the three year cycle as well as select pieces for weddings and funerals. If you don't see the one you need, reach out and I probably have a copy I can send.

Worship Songs

What makes my music unique?

Accessible

My number one goal is to make music that engages parishioners. Here you'll find melodies that empower each member to be fully present in worship. Music that is simple to learn, fun to sing, and easy to remember.

Musical

Whether your group is a full choir, worship band, or solo piano; each piece is scalable to your needs. Vocal harmonies and diverse styles give you the opportunity to make each song as simple or complex as you and your group desire.

Powerful

The liturgy of the Mass is beautiful. From "My God, my God, why have you abandoned me?" to "Let the Lord enter; he is king of glory.", these settings capture the strength, the anguish, and the hope behind every word.

Personal

I strive for every piece to be easily integrated into your worship. However, if you don't find what you're looking for, or want a piece transposed to a different key, please reach out and let me know how I can help.
